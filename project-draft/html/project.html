My Nutrition Tracker


Problem/Data Domain

Many people have health problems related to eating, and our group members can all personally relate to the topic of nutrition and diet.Currently, there are no applications that provide the localization of data/easy to visualize on a platform that comprehends information of ingredients for all meals for general users. We believe that an application that consists of all eating information and habits together would be beneficial to any user. MyFitnessPal focuses on calories and food groups but doesn’t focus on previous/current eating history. Mint Health(薄荷健康 ) is similar that it records calories but not ingredients. Our application would provide inclusion to a wider range of users who can have access to more functionalities than similar platforms. By focusing on ingredients and by recording all meals eaten, we hope that the app can reveal a pattern over time for the user’s own health benefit. We plan on looking for an AI that could assist in complex analysis. Having a platform that could have all this information and goals in one place can be beneficial for someone’s physical and mental health.

App Description

The app will allow users to add foods and recipes on different dates and evaluate their eating habits and goals. Users will be able to change views for daily, weekly, monthly, or quarterly goals. The user base will be a diverse group of people who are interested in nutrition and diet management. Through the app's AI-powered nutrition plan, users will be able to build a personalized this plan based on their needs and preferences.

The application's primary purpose is to help people with digestive issues or those who are health-conscious to keep track of what they are eating and maintain a digital record of it for future reference. The app will be accessible to anyone interested in nutrition and diet management, and it will positively impact racial minority groups by providing them with access to information and tools to monitor their eating habits. For example, lactose persistence is an issue that affects Asian communities, and the app can help users identify and manage this condition. Overall, the app's goal is to promote healthy eating habits and make it easy for users to manage their diet and nutrition.

  • Our app is designed to make tracking meals and ingredients simple and effective. Users can easily input the ingredients for every meal they eat, allowing them to keep a record of their food intake. In addition to tracking individual meals, the app also features a calendar that provides an overview of previous meals.
  • One of the standout features of our app is the ability to store and share recipes. Users can create a list of ingredients for each recipe and share it with the community. Other users can then upvote the recipe and add it to their own meal record calendar. This makes it easy for users to incorporate new recipes into their routine and discover new meal ideas.
  • To help users stay on track with their eating habits and goals, our app includes a checklist of food categories that users can customize. This allows users to create a personalized list of food categories to fulfill in a week. In addition, the app provides daily, weekly, and monthly insights into goals and ingredients eaten, giving users a comprehensive view of their eating habits. To make it easy to track specific dietary needs and preferences, the app allows users to tag and label their food. This includes options for probiotics, vegetables, fruit, and minimum amounts. The app also provides recommendations based on recording ideal eating times based on an user’s goals and dietary restrictions and guidelines based on goals. Users can easily edit and customize their recipes, making it simple to adjust their meals based on their needs and preferences.

Our app provides users with a comprehensive tool to track and optimize their eating habits, making it easier to reach their health and wellness goals
